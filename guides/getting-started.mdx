## Welcome to Softbee

Softbee is a comprehensive beekeeping management application designed to help you track and manage your apiaries, beehives, inspections, and inventory all in one place.

## First Time Setup

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create Your Account">
    Download the Softbee app and create your account. You'll need to provide:
    - Email address
    - Password
    - Basic profile information
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create Your First Apiary">
    After logging in, you'll be prompted to create your first apiary. An apiary is a location where you keep your beehives.
    
    **Required Information:**
    - **Apiary Name**: Give your apiary a descriptive name (minimum 3 characters)
      - Example: "Apiario El Prado" or "Mountain View Apiary"
    
    **Optional Information:**
    - **Location**: Enter the physical location of your apiary
      - Example: "Vereda La Esperanza, Cota"
    - **Treatments**: Toggle whether you apply treatments when bees are sick
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add Your First Beehive">
    Once your apiary is created, you can start adding beehives to track individual colonies.
    
    Navigate to your apiary dashboard and select **Monitoring** → **Colmena** to manage your beehives.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Explore the Dashboard">
    Familiarize yourself with the main features:
    - **Apiaries**: View and manage all your apiary locations
    - **Beehives**: Track individual hive health and status
    - **Monitoring**: Record inspections and observations
    - **Inventory**: Manage your beekeeping supplies and equipment
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Understanding the App Structure

### Hierarchy

Softbee organizes your beekeeping data in a simple hierarchy:

```
Account
  └── Apiaries (Locations)
      └── Beehives (Individual Colonies)
          └── Inspections & Data
```

<Note>
  You can manage multiple apiaries from a single account, making it easy to track operations across different locations.
</Note>

## Navigation Tips

### Quick Actions

- **Back Navigation**: Use the back arrow in the top-left corner to return to previous screens
- **Add New Items**: Look for the `+` or "Add" buttons throughout the app
- **Edit Existing Data**: Tap the edit icon on any card to modify information

### Finding Your Data

- All apiaries are accessible from the main dashboard
- Select an apiary to view its specific beehives and inventory
- Use the monitoring section to access inspection tools and questions

<Tip>
  The app uses a clean architecture pattern, ensuring your data is securely stored and synchronized across devices.
</Tip>
